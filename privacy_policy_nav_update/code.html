Your data is your discipline. We are committed to protecting your privacy with a zero-collection architecture. Your progress remains entirely your own.

01. Introduction 02. Zero Data Collection 03. Your Rights

01. Introduction

Welcome to 75 HARD ELITE. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information on the 75 HARD ELITE platform.

We believe that true mental toughness requires total ownership. That's why we've built our platform to be a completely local, user-end application. We don't just protect your data; we refuse to touch it.

02. Zero Data Collection

75 HARD ELITE is designed as a standalone environment. Our philosophy is simple: No tracking, no syncing, no exceptions.

"LOCAL STORAGE ONLY."

All data—including your workout logs, progress photos, biometric markers, and personal notes—is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This application does not possess a backend database or external server sync functionality.

  • No Remote Servers: Your information never leaves your device. We do not collect, store, or share any user data on our own infrastructure.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking pixels or behavioral analytics tools. Your habits are your business.
  • No Accounts: Since there is no cloud synchronization, you are not required to create a centralized account to use the application features.

03. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally, you have absolute control. You can delete all your progress and personal information at any time by clearing your application data or deleting the local database via your browser/device settings. If you have questions about this local-first architecture, please reach out.
